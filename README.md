## What I build

Product tooling that compresses execution. PRD generators, prioritisation frameworks, stakeholder update systems. The boring 
stuff that actually gets used.

Music making tools. Polyrhythm generators, digital oscillators and effects, Ableton extension. The cool stuff that's fun to do and purely for my specific needs.

## How I work

For product stuff I use the tools the way I work with a good APM: feed it the right context at the 
right time, let it execute, keep the judgement calls for myself. For audio tooling, its all about iteration and pushing boundaries of my own capabilities and that of the hardware synths I use.
